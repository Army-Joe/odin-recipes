Caesar Salad Dressing

The Outback Steakhouse Caesar salad dressing

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Olive Oil
  • 6 Large Eggs
  • 1/3 Cup Red Wine Vinegar
  • 2 1/2 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Parsley
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Garlic
  • 2 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp. Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 2 Tbsp. Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Salt, pepper, dry mustard &, anchovy filets to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend on low for 20 seconds to incorporate oil.
  3. Blend on high for 20 more seconds to mix thoroughly.
  4. Place in a covered container, and refrigerate.

Notes

Dressing will keep in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.
